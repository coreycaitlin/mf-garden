## Basic information
- **Scientific name:** Oxalis oregana
- **Plant type:** Perennial/Groundcover
- **USDA zones:** 7-9
- **Native region:** Pacific Northwest, from Washington to California

## Growth characteristics
- **Mature height:** 4-8 inches
- **Mature spread:** Spreading indefinitely via rhizomes
- **Growth rate:** Medium to Fast
- **Lifespan:** Long-lived perennial

## Growing conditions
- **Sun requirements:** Part Shade/Full Shade
- **Water needs:** Medium
- **Soil type:** Rich, humusy, moist
- **Soil pH:** 5.0-6.5 (acidic)

## Seasonal interest
- **Bloom time:** March-June
- **Bloom color:** White
- **Fall color:** Deciduous (may persist in mild winters)
- **Winter interest:** Limited - foliage may die back
