# Pacific Wax Myrtle

## Basic information
- **Scientific name:** Myrica californica
- **Plant type:** Evergreen Shrub
- **USDA zones:** 7-10
- **Native region:** Pacific Coast, from Washington to California

## Growth characteristics
- **Mature height:** 15 feet
- **Mature spread:** 8-12 feet
- **Growth rate:** Medium-Fast
- **Lifespan:** Long-lived shrub

## Growing conditions
- **Sun requirements:** Full Sun/Part Shade (grows best with some sunlight but tolerates almost full shade)
- **Water needs:** Low-Medium (drought tolerant once established)
- **Soil type:** Adaptable; tolerates poor, sandy soils
- **Soil pH:** 5.5-7.0

## Seasonal interest
- **Bloom time:** Spring
- **Bloom color:** Inconspicuous
- **Fall color:** Evergreen
- **Winter interest:** Yes - attractive evergreen foliage year-round

## Wildlife value
- **Attracts:** Birds
- **Host plant for:** N/A
- **Provides:** Berries for birds; cover and nesting sites
